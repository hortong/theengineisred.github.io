Vision-first branding, sighted or not.

A collaboration with Guide Dogs for the Blind.

Creating a brand that not only communicated the Guide Dogs for the Blind team’s mission, but embodied it for the entire Guide Dogs for the Blind family, regardless of sight level.

Together we were able to evolve the brand in a way that equally embraced innovations in accessibility and non-visual communication with stunning and modern visual branding. Combining elements such as texture and technology with innovations in low-vision typography and accessibility technology.

Creating a single set of materials that equally serve and delight students, volunteers, staff, and donors is helping to unify our entire team, while reinforcing our core mission. Looking forward, we hope to push the industry and the world’s perception of what accessible design can truly be.
