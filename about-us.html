More than the sum of our parts.

A match, a gas can. A client, an agency. Each has value, but together, in controlled chaos, they can become significantly more.

Producing great creative work isn’t easy. It takes a tremendous amount of talent, dedication, passion, trust, and experience. But man, is it worth it. Since our founding, we’ve strived to build an agency where the world’s best creatives thrive. We fight for our culture, our team, and our work, and we love it. Together we are the Engine, and we are just getting started.

Monthly Mix Tapes

Every month, we each create a studio soundtrack and share it with all you lucky people. You can find some of our favorites below, and find them all here.

  • Earning the right to be heard.

    We believe that digging into the trenches, getting to know the consumer, understanding the business model, getting your hands dirty is where the best ideas come from. We commit to coming in learning, open minded, and inquisitive. First becoming part of the client team, then working together to create innovation and change.

  • Cherish the classics, embrace innovation.

    We are constantly amazed at how fast technology and media are evolving and together how they are explosively expanding opportunities to reach people. But we love billboards and direct mail, and picking up the phone and saying hello. We often find the best results come from creating a healthy mix of tried and true and bold and new.

  • Nothing’s sacred.

    We believe that skepticism is healthy. Questing why, asking what if, going past what has been established can reinforce the strongest of ideas and evolve others. From our creative to our process, we commit to constantly question, test, evolve and learn.

  • Create don’t iterate.

    We believe the best ideas are yet to be had. That there is not only the opportunity, but the mandate to create. Its is this passion that drives us. Great creative requires too much effort, too much risk, too much investment to merely iterate existing ideas or one-up a competitor. We strive to go beyond what is, and focus on what can be.

  • Follow the idea.

    We believe in following inspiration wherever it may lead. We believe in learning and forming hypotheses, in testing and changing, in dreaming and experimenting. We commit to creating an environment that allows for the flexibility, resources, and collaboration to explore big ideas, test assumptions, and create great things.

  • Healthy creatives create great work.

    We believe that in order to consistently and effectively create great work at the studio, creatives must live full lives outside the studio. Going beyond work-life balance. Investing in the emotional and physical health of our team. Facilitating a fun, intimate, and trusting team dynamic. Encouraging and nurturing creatives to live inspired, full, and healthy lives. The best work comes not from pulling from, rather from investing in passionate people.

Over the past seven years we’ve had the honor to collaborate with amazing brands, build an incredible team, and bring the dream of the Engine to life. With the launch of our Minneapolis studio, the expansion of our interactive team, and the evolution into Agile, we stand at a unique and exciting crossroads. We are just getting started.

The Engine is a creative agency looking for a ridiculously smart copywriter to join our growing team.
